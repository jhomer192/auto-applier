# T1 Referral Pack — Jack Homer

A referred application has roughly **10× the callback rate** of a cold one. For each
target company below: a curated set of LinkedIn search URLs (so you can find current
employees in your stack to DM) plus a drafted outreach message you can adapt.

**The DM strategy:**
1. Don't ask for a referral up front. Ask for a quick read on the role.
2. Lead with something specific you've built that maps to their work.
3. Keep it under 4 sentences. They're busy.
4. ~30% of replies will offer a referral unprompted. The other ~30% will give the role honest signal worth more than a referral.

**Your unfair edges to mine:**
- **VT CS alumni** — search any company + "Virginia Tech" filter
- **C3 AI alumni** — many ex-C3 folks are at AI startups now; search any company + "C3 AI"
- **Forward-deployed peers** — most FDEs love talking to other FDEs; you speak their language

## How to use this pack

1. Open **5 referral targets** at one company. Total time: 5 minutes.
2. Customize the `[Name]` and one specific hook (their public talk, blog post, or repo). 10 minutes.
3. Send all 5 DMs in a single sitting. ~30% reply rate, so expect 1–2 useful conversations per company.
4. **Don't ask for a referral on the first message.** Ask for color on the role. Most who like you will offer a referral unprompted.
5. Track responses in a simple spreadsheet (or just in Telegram with the bot's history).
